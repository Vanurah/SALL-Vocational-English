Peer Feedback

Get feedback from a classmate

Peer feedback helps you notice things you might miss on your own. Pair up with a classmate, swap your Reflection Log or worksheet answers, and email each other comments.

How to give good peer feedback: Point out one thing your classmate did well, one thing to improve, and one suggestion — always in a respectful, encouraging tone.
