Before You Begin

User guide

⏱️ How long does it take?

About 20–30 minutes per session, 2–3 times a week. Short and consistent beats long and rare.

📱 What do I need?

Just a smartphone, computer, or tablet with internet access. No app installation required.

🧭 What's the order?

Vocabulary → Grammar → Self-Check → Reflection. Repeat weekly, moving up a stage when you're ready.

❓ Stuck on something?

Reach a teacher directly by email or WhatsApp on the Feedback & Teachers page — no need to wait for class.
